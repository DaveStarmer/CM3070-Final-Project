Camera System Deployment Instructions

Subscribe to alerts

  1. Search for 'SNS' in AWS search bar and select
  2. Select 'topics' from the left hand side
  3. Select the only topic shown
  4. Copy the ARN
  5. Click on the yellow "Create Subscription" button
  6. Paste the ARN into the top field
  7. Select 'Email' from the protocol list
  8. Enter the email address to subscribe in the appropriate field
  9. Check your email - you will need to follow the instructions in the confirmation email
